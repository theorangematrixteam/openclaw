# Target Audience ICP — Orange Matrix Outbound

## Ideal Client Profile (ICP)
We are looking for **early-stage, growing D2C brands** in India that are in the "expansion" phase. We avoid established, massive brands (too much red tape/agency lock-in) and focus on brands that have recently launched or recently raised seed/Series A funding.

### Core Verticals
- **Fashion & Streetwear** (e.g., Snitch, DEFKIDD)
- **Beauty & Skincare** (e.g., Antinorm, Pilgrim, Unfltrd)
- **Lifestyle & Accessories** (e.g., Jewellery, Athleisure, Home Decor)
- **Premium/Luxury D2C** (e.g., The Orikai, Araiya)

### Key Characteristics
- **Stage:** Seed to Series A.
- **Status:** Recently launched (within 1-2 years) OR recently raised funding.
- **Social Presence:** Growing Instagram/social presence but likely lacks consistent, high-quality, scaled content strategy.
- **Pain Point:** Needs professional social media management, content production (reels, visuals), and community building to capitalize on their growth/funding.
- **Geographic Focus:** India-based.

## Outreach Angle
"We specialize in helping emerging D2C brands scale their social presence through high-conversion visuals and strategic management, specifically for brands at your stage of growth."
