## Unified Content Strategy for Orange Matrix

### Format Mix (This Month - Awareness)
- **70% Reels** — Attention-grabbing demos, transformations
- **20% Carousels** — Educational content, quick tips
- **10% Static** — Hero shots, announcements

### Reel Structure (All Reels)
```
[0-3s] Hook: Bold visual + statement
[3-10s] Context: Problem/solution setup
[10-25s] Demo: Show the transformation
[25-35s] Payoff: Deliver the promise
[35-45s] CTA: Clear call-to-action
```

### Hook Patterns (Rotate These)
1. **"Stop doing X, do this instead"** — Problem-focused
2. **"Nobody talks about this workflow. It changes everything."** — Knowledge gap
3. **"If you're a brand owner struggling with. . ."** — Specific callout
4. **"This $X makes your content look 10x expensive"** — Bold claim
5. **"5 things I learned after [result]"** — Personal experience
6. **"Most people [common mistake]. Here's the fix."** — Problem-solution

### Content Pillars (April - Awareness Focus)
1. **Visual Transformation** — Before/After brand content
2. **Platform Demos** — How to use Orange Matrix on Instagram/LinkedIn
3. **Tool Tutorials** — Quick tips for better content
4. **BTS Execution** — What real work looks like
5. **Results & Proof** — Client wins, metrics, case previews

### CTAs (All Posts)
- Primary: Link in bio / Book a call
- Secondary: "Save this for later" / "Follow for more"
- Engagement: "Comment X and we'll DM you the guide"
- Never: Multiple competing CTAs

### Visual Consistency Checklist
- [ ] White 70%, Orange 30% color ratio
- [ ] Clean, premium aesthetic
- [ ] Bold, high-contrast text overlays
- [ ] Product/brand is the hero
- [ ] Consistent theme across all posts
- [ ] No generic "agency brochure" feel
- [ ] Professional but not stiff

### Posting Cadence
- **1 post/day** across all platforms
- **Instagram:** Visual demos, BTS, reels, carousels
- **LinkedIn:** Case studies with business angle
- **X:** Quick takes, tool drops, commentary
- **YouTube Shorts:** BTS clips, quick tips
