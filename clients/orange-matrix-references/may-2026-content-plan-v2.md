## Visual Style Guide (Based on References)

### Color Usage
- White 70%, Orange 30% accent
- Black text for body
- High contrast for hooks

### Typography
- Bold sans-serif for hooks (like Mobile Editing Club)
- Clean, readable for body text
- Maximum 2 font weights per post

### Layout
- Hook = visual anchor (top 30%)
- Content = middle (50%)
- CTA = bottom (20%)
- Eye path: Top → Center → Bottom

### Image Style
- Product-first framing (Rainfrog.ai)
- Warm editorial tones or clean minimal
- Before/After for proof posts
- BTS screen recordings for process
