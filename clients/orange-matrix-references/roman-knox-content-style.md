## Content Style Analysis

### Format
- **Primary:** Carousel posts (7 slides typically)
- **Hook:** Strong headline on first slide + short video intro
- **Structure:** Numbered list format (e.g., "1. Front-end design", "2. Figma to Code")
- **Visual style:** Clean, dark theme, magazine/editorial aesthetic

### Design Language
- **Color palette:** Dark backgrounds (black/dark gray) with vibrant accent colors
- **Typography:** Bold headers, clean sans-serif body text
- **Layout:** Card-based carousel with consistent page indicators ("Page 2/7 swipe")
- **Branding:** Consistent "Instagram Roman.Knox" footer on each slide

### Content Themes
1. **AI + Design tools integration** (Claude Skills, Figma workflows)
2. **Developer productivity tips**
3. **Design system best practices**
4. **Tutorial/educational content** with clear actionable steps

### Hook Patterns
- Numbered lists: "7 Claude Skills That Will Change Your Design Workflow"
- Problem-solution format: "Turn Figma designs into production-ready code"
- Action-oriented: "Access 10 polished themes..."

### Engagement
- **Likes:** 11,759+ on carousel posts
- **Video views:** 53,000+ on intro videos
- **Format:** 5-second video intro + carousel = high retention

### CTA Style
- Subtle CTAs embedded in content ("swipe" indicators)
- No aggressive "follow me" or "link in bio" callouts
- Value-first approach: teach something, build trust

## What Works Well

1. **Educational value:** Each post teaches something actionable
2. **Consistent visual identity:** Dark theme, bold typography, card layouts
3. **Numbered lists:** Easy to consume, clear progression
4. **Tool-focused:** Specific tools/commands make it practical
5. **Clean CTAs:** `npx skills add https://github.com/...` - copy-paste ready
