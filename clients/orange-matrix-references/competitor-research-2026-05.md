## Content Patterns from Research

### What's Working in 2026:
1. **Carousels outperform reels** for saves and engagement
2. **"Stop doing X" hooks** — pattern interrupt, highly effective
3. **Before/After transformations** — visual proof
4. **"X vs Y" comparisons** — easy to consume, shareable
5. **Tool reveals with gatekeeping** — show tools but not full process
6. **Systems over tools** — teaching workflows, not just features
7. **Authority positioning** — "Here's what actually works"
8. **Specific numbers** — "5 hooks," "7 creators," "10 minutes"

### Orange Matrix Differentiation:
- **Not just AI** — We do execution, not just generation
- **D2C focus** — Fashion, jewelry, product brands
- **Systems + Creation** — Both strategy and delivery
- **One-stop** — Not just visuals, also social management
