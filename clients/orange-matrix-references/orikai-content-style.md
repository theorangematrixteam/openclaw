## Content Style Analysis

### Format
- **Primary:** Carousel posts (5 slides typical)
- **Secondary:** Single image posts, video reels
- **Hook:** Emotional messaging with minimal text on visuals
- **Structure:** Story-based, soft sell

### Design Language
- **Color palette:** Warm, neutral tones (beige, cream, soft pastels)
- **Typography:** Minimal text on images, long captions
- **Layout:** Clean product photography, lifestyle shots
- **Visual style:** Premium, heirloom quality aesthetic

### Content Themes
1. **Emotional parenting** - "Every child deserves to feel: I belong. I matter. I am enough."
2. **Product storytelling** - Positioning as heirloom pieces, not fast fashion
3. **Founder journey** - Behind-the-scenes startup journey posts (Yashvi - Orikai founder)
4. **Lifestyle aesthetics** - Vacation outfits, everyday style for kids
5. **Subtle product drops** - "Dropping soon. Trust me you'll want it first"

### Hook Patterns

- **Curiosity hooks:** "Not everyone will have this. That's the point."
- **Founder vulnerability:** "It's been 8 months since we've been working on Orikai..."
- **Soft announcements:** "Dropping soon. And trust me you'll want it first"

### Caption Style

- **Tone:** Warm, intimate, thoughtful
- **Structure:** Story → Insight → Subtle CTA
- **Hashtags:** Minimal, relevant only (#contentcreator #toddlerofinstagram #kidsfashion)
