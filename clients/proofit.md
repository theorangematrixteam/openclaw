## Core Business

- **Brand:** Proofit / PROOFIT
- **Category:** Residential property inspection / home inspection
- **What it does:** Independent third-party inspection of residential properties
- **Geography:** Mumbai-based
- **Core outcome:** Help buyers, homeowners, builders, investors, and NRIs make informed property decisions with a clear evidence-based understanding of a property’s true condition

## Core Offer

Proofit provides:

- residential property inspection
- snag / defect reporting
- thermal-imaging-backed inspection
- engineering-led evaluation
- decision support before handover, occupancy, purchase, or investment

Website-backed service directions include:

- new flat possession inspection
- resale property inspection
- water leakage inspection
- pre-renovation and post-renovation inspection

## Key Differentiators

Based on the website, Proofit differentiates through:

- independent third-party evaluations
- AI-enabled thermal imaging technology
- engineering-driven inspections
- photo- and thermal-backed reports
- focus on visible and hidden defects
- adaptation of international / North American inspection standards to Indian construction realities
