# Notch India

## Core Business

- **Brand:** Notch India
- **Category:** Women’s clothing / fashion brand
- **What it does:** Creates fancy and stylish clothes for women
- **Core outcome:** Help women look stylish and feel included across age groups

## Core Brand Context

- brand new brand
- wants to grow big in women’s fashion
- aspiration shared by client: become like **Snitch for women**
- age-inclusive direction matters; not only youth-focused fashion

## Positioning Direction

Early positioning direction inferred from Jinay’s context:

- stylish women’s fashion
- age-inclusive appeal
- modern but wearable
- aspirational without becoming alienating to older customers

This should not be positioned as:

- ultra-young-only trend fashion
- generic ethnic/fusion clutter
- luxury in a stiff, inaccessible way

## Target Customer Direction

Current broad direction:

- women who want fancy / stylish clothing
- women who still want comfort and wearability
- women across age groups, including older customers

Important note:

- the brand should not visually communicate that it is only for very young women

## Content Implications

Content should likely balance:

- product showcase
- styling / outfit imagination
- aspirational brand vibe
- age inclusivity
- collection storytelling
- launch / hype building

## Special-Day / Seasonal Relevance

Reasonable relevant moments include:

- Earth Day or sustainability only if the brand actually has a sustainability angle
- women-focused occasions when genuinely aligned
- festive / seasonal wardrobe transitions
- collection drops / launches
- gifting or occasion-wear windows when relevant

Do not force irrelevant special-day content.

- stylish
- fresh

- inclusive

## Notes For Future Agents

When planning for Notch India:

- do not treat it like generic women’s fashion
- preserve the age-inclusive angle
- use reference brands for study, not cloning
- plan content that makes the brand feel wearable, stylish, and launch-worthy
