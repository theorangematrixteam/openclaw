## 💡 Key Visual Elements to Replicate for Proofit

To execute the **"Proof-led storytelling"** angle, the Reel should incorporate:

1. **The "Hidden Story" Macro:** Use extreme close-ups of damp patches, seepage marks, or cracks in old walls. These act as the "characters" in our story.
2. **Layered Textures:** Show the contrast between the "heritage" (beautiful old architecture) and the "reality" (structural decay).
3. **The Reveal:** Start with a beautiful wide shot of a heritage property, then transition/cut to the "hidden" issues found during an inspection.
4. **Educational Overlays:** Use clean, minimal text overlays to point out specific issues (e.g., "Evidence of 10-year-old seepage," "Structural crack formation").
5. **Mood Transition:** Transition from a nostalgic/calm audio vibe (Heritage/History) to a more serious/alert tone (Inspection/Risk) as the damage is revealed.
