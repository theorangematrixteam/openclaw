# Jinay's Visual Taste Profile

- **Scope**: global
- **Agent**: sarah
- **Created**: 2026-04-25
- **Updated**: 2026-04-25
- **Status**: verified

## LIKES ✅
- Bright sunlight, golden hour, natural daylight
- High contrast, saturated colors
- Nature backdrops (ocean, trees, mountains)
- Low angles, dramatic perspectives
- Sparkle, lens flare, light play
- Confident subjects, strong poses
- Editorial/fashion photography style
- Clean backgrounds, minimal clutter
- Texture detail (fabric, skin, hair)
- Warm tones over cool tones

## DISLIKES ❌
- Flat lighting, overcast moods
- Pastel/muted color palettes
- Studio setups with plain backgrounds
- Flat lays / overhead product shots
- Marble surfaces, cold grey environments
- AI artifacts (extra limbs, distorted faces, text overlay)
- Oversaturated neon/HDR look
- Foggy/hazy washed-out appearance
- Film grain aesthetic (when unintended)
- Cartoon/anime style

## Quality Standards
- Photorealistic is the baseline — should look like real photos
- No visible AI artifacts in final deliverables
- Sharp detail, not soft/blurry
- Natural skin tones, not plastic
- Fabric should show real texture and drape
- Colors should be vivid but not oversaturated/neon
