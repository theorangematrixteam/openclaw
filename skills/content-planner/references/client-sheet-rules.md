# Client Sheet Rules

## Purpose

Client sheets are for planning and approval.

They are not for internal production tracking.

## Keep in client sheets

- dates
- content type
- bucket
- hook/topic
- concept/objective
- post angle
- CTA
- approval-facing status if needed

## Keep out of client sheets

- internal backlog management
- cross-client visibility
- billing status
- invoice tracking
- internal ops notes that should not be shared

## Sheet improvement rule

If a client sheet is weak, improve it toward the stronger planning standard.
Do not rebuild everything from scratch unless needed.

## Orange Matrix context

Recurring client plans should be strong enough that they can feed directly into the internal `content tracker` afterward.
