### Technical Workflows
- **Up-scaling:** Successfully upscaled approved images to 4096px via SeedVR2.
- **Masking/Cropping:**
    - Encountered issue where `InpaintCropImproved` merged multiple subjects into one crop.
    - Developed `run_crop_per_mask.py`:
        1. Runs SAM3 segmentation.
        2. Splits the combined mask into individual masks using `scipy.ndimage.label`.
        3. Runs InpaintCrop separately for each kid's clothing.
    - Result: Individual crops for each child, ready for outfit swapping.

### Visual Taste / Quality Rules (Updated)
- **Caucasian subjects** for Totsburg.
- **The "Torso Clearance" Rule:** T-shirt chest/abdomen must be 80%+ unobstructed. No hands covering mouth/chest.
- **Outward-Facing Dynamics:** Movement (waving, pointing) directed AWAY from the garment.
- **Interaction over Action:** Focus on relationship/shared laughter, not solo "stunt" poses.
- **Contact Shadows:** Ensure ambient occlusion at feet/floor to prevent "floating" look.
- **Fabric Texture:** Soft directional lighting to ensure 3D folds in fabric.
