# Proofit Design Style Guide

## Brand Colors
- **Primary Orange** — Used for hooks, keywords, warnings, CTAs, and brand accent
- **White** — Background canvas, clean and high-end
- **Black/Dark Grey** — Body text, secondary information

## Format
- **All posts: 1080x1350** (Instagram 4:5 portrait)
- **Carousels:** Multiple slides at 1080x1350

## Typography Rules
- **Extreme weight contrast:** Massive bold sans-serif headers (often orange) paired with lighter regular-weight secondary text (black/dark grey)
- **Text as graphic element:** Headlines are visual anchors, not just reading material
- **Hierarchy is king:** Eye path is always Top → Center → Bottom
- **No more than 1-2 text elements per slide** — the rest is breathing room

## Layout Rules
- **Intentional negative space:** Massive white space, but every element is surgically placed
- **No clutter:** No unnecessary borders, shadows, generic stock photos overlapping text
- **No over-decoration:** Design relies on typography + color alone
- **Information density is LOW per slide** — one idea per slide maximum

## Carousel Structure
1. **Slide 1 (The Hook):** High tension, large aggressive text. Problem statement or warning. Orange dominates.
2. **Middle Slides (The Insight):** Breakdown of the problem. More text but still strict hierarchy. Information delivered in short punchy lines.
3. **Final Slide (CTA):** Simple, clean, direct. "DM 'INSPECT'" or similar. Orange CTA on white.

## Content Voice
- **Blunt, direct, almost journalistic** — no marketing fluff
- **No AI-speak:** No em dashes in headers, no "elevate", no "transform", no rule-of-three lists
- **Tone:** Authoritative but accessible. Like a trusted inspector telling you what brokers won't.

## Post Types (from 16-post analysis)
- **Single-slide posts:** One powerful statement or fact
- **3-slide carousels:** Hook → Insight → CTA
- **5-7 slide carousels:** Hook → Series of facts/breakdown → CTA
- **Mixed carousels:** Some slides are text-only, some include property photos or inspection imagery

## File Structure
- Each post lives in its own numbered folder (1-16)
- Files named by slide number (1.png, 2.png, etc.)
- All PNG format, 1080x1350

## Key Differentiator from "Generic"
- The design is **text-first, not decoration-first**
- Orange is used as a **weapon** (for emphasis), not as wallpaper
- The white space is **earned** — it makes the orange text hit harder
- Carousels tell a **story** with escalating tension, not random facts
