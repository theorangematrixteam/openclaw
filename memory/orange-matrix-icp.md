# Orange Matrix — Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

## Target Audience
Early-stage product brands (clothing, jewelry, D2C products) that need content + social media presence but don't have in-house teams.

## Who They Are
- Recently launched brands (startups, indie brands, small D2C)
- Product-first founders — good at making stuff, weak at marketing/content
- Need a one-stop solution: visuals, social management, creative direction, execution
- Don't have (and shouldn't hire) a full in-house content team yet

## What They Need
- High-conversion visuals (product shots, lifestyle content)
- Social media presence and management
- Creative direction (they have product, not brand strategy)
- End-to-end execution — they want one partner, not 5 freelancers

## Why Orange Matrix
- One-stop brand growth company — everything under one roof
- Works with brands that can't afford a full team but need full capability
- We handle: visuals, social, creative direction, production, execution

## Where to Find Them
- Instagram / social-first brands
- D2C marketplaces
- Startup communities
- New brand launches in fashion, jewelry, consumer products
